Our digital vision

Lambeth has set out bold strategic imperatives in challenging times

Our digital vision is to be a connected, inclusive place to thrive. Where everyone has access to excellent services, enabled by modern technology and ways of working.

  • 1

    Enable growth and development, to provide opportunities for residents to improve their life chances, and protect our core revenue streams to help our most vulnerable

  • 2

    Ensure benefits of growth increase community resilience, and that we target investment at preventative services

  • 3

    Reform the way we commission and deliver services with our partners to provide and promote care and independence

  • 4

    Make sure Lambeth is a place where people want to live, work and invest

To deliver these we must change the way we work to meet raised expectations. We must be enabled by fit-for-purpose technology and processes to remain relevant in the internet-era.

Becoming a 21st Century organisation

Becoming a 21st century organisation will enable Lambeth to put citizens, businesses and communities at the centre of our services to deliver the best experience possible.

  • Users spend less time using the service

  • Users find that there is always an option that meets their needs

  • Users stay independent for as long as they can

  • Users always find the service helpful, and receive help at the right time

  • Users find the service easy to use and understand

Connected

Creating spaces and areas to gather, build relationships, communicate and unite - online or in person, for everyone - to enable growth and development in Lambeth.

Community cohesion will be very important for the future otherwise the community will become fragmented
Being connected means less rivalry and more understanding and concern about each other ensuring that we all benefit

Inclusive

People are at the heart of all decisions made, we must understand all of their needs, act in kind, and be caring and open always - to help make Lambeth a place people want to live, work, invest and thrive.

Not leaving people behind or out. The services have to be for everybody regardless of your background
It’s about communication and transparency about what the council is capable of delivering, the difficulties they face and why they are not acting

Everyone

Every resident, business, community, council worker. They are our users, lets work with them and represent them all to promote care and independence.

There’s lots of people and organisations already doing things [providing services] it’s about getting them involved properly

Services

Providing services that work, are fit for purpose, readily available and enjoyable. We will target our investment to increase resilience in Lambeth.

Services where everyone feels welcomed and cared about...where people's cultural backgrounds are considered
We can’t expect people to come to services, services need to also go to them, especially the young people

Modern technology

Technology that is informed by user-need, is design led, and is delivered iteratively. It will be open, scalable, adaptable, easily maintainable, user led, and less reliant on single third party suppliers. The technology of Lambeth will enable modern ways of working.

Modern ways of working

Modern ways of working encompasses the structures we work in, the processes we work with, the tools we work with, and the way in which we deliver our work.
