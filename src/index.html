Our digital vision

Lambeth has set out bold strategic imperatives in challenging times

Lambeth has set out bold strategic imperatives in challenging times:

  • 1

    Enable growth and development, to provide opportunities for residents to improve their life chances, and protect our core revenue streams to help our most vulnerable

  • 2

    Ensure benefits of growth increase community resilience, and that we target investment at preventative services

  • 3

    Reform the way we commission and deliver services with our partners to provide and promote care and independence

  • 4

    Make sure Lambeth is a place where people want to live, work and invest

To deliver these we must change the way we work to meet raised expectations. We must be enabled by fit-for-purpose technology and processes to remain relevant in the internet-era.

Becoming a 21st Century organisation

Becoming a 21st century organisation will enable Lambeth to put citizens, businesses and communities at the centre of our services to deliver the best experience possible.

  • Users spend less time using the service

  • Users find that there is always an option that meets their needs

  • Users stay independent for as long as they can

  • Users always find the service helpful, and receive help at the right time

  • Users find the service easy to use and understand

Our digital vision

Is to be a connected, inclusive place to thrive. Where everyone has access to excellent services, enabled by modern technology and ways of working.
